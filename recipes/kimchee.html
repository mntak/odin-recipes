Kim Chee Fried Rice

Description

Kimchee Fried Rice is absolutely fantastic! It's a type of Korean fried rice and is cooked with lots of kimchi. To give it more texture and flavor a variety of meat and/or vegetables are often added.

So, when I make my kimchi fried rice I typically add spam and eggs. It's oh so tasty.

Kimchi fried rice is one of the most popular ways to use up your old ripened kimchi.

Ingredients

  1. Kimchee
  2. Gochujang
  3. Egg
  4. Spam
  5. Green onion
  6. Nori
  7. Butter
  8. Sesame Oil
  9. Cooked rice

Steps

  1. Pour some sesame oil in the pan
  2. Fry the kimchee until it softens
  3. Add in the spam
  4. Mix in the gochujang and rice
  5. Fry an egg sunny side up
  6. Cut the nori and mix in the rice
  7. Lay the egg on top and you're done!
